Notion Connection

The Between the Covers database is already set up in your Notion. You just need to paste your integration token below to connect the app to it.

How to get your token

1. Go to notion.so/my-integrations

2. Click New integration

3. Name it "Book Scanner" and save

4. Copy the token (starts with secret_)

5. Open the Between the Covers database in Notion

6. Click ··· → Connections → connect "Book Scanner"

7. Paste the token above and hit Save
